Photography

Art, Science, Hobby

Photography, what does that word mean to you? Does it bring up images in your mind of family vaction photos taken when you were a kid? Maybe you are reminded of all those profesionally taken Christmas photos for holiday greeting cards. Or maybe you were just reminded of those very old burlesque tintypes that are stashed in a safe place. Photography colors our world, every picture has a story. Photography is a facinating mixture of art, science, and the desire to capture a precise second in time. It can be as simple or as complex as the person taking the picture wants it to be.

Chinese proverb says "One picture is worth ten thousand words."
